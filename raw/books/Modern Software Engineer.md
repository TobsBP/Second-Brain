In 19/04/2026 i started to read the book Engenharia de Software Moderna from Marco Tulio Valente.

The first thing that i notice, was about the Brooks concepts: **complexity**, **conformity**, **easy changes**, **invisible**. And its some things that i never thought about, if the law changes,
or the company ideology, and so on.

Another thing is the **functional** and **not-functional** requirements for a software. The functional is the action that the software **needs to do**, like view your account, or change plan. And the **not-functional** are those that the people don't see, like speed, performance, security.
