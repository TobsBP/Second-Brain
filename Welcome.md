# Welcome to your LLM Wiki

This vault is maintained by Claude Code. Here's what I can do for you.

---

## Ingest

**Journal entries** — Drop a markdown file into `raw/journal/` and say "ingest it". I'll read it, discuss the key takeaways with you, then update your personal wiki pages: goals, habits, health, patterns, relationships — whatever came up in the entry.

**Book chapters** — Drop a chapter into `raw/books/<book-name>/` and say "ingest chapter N". I'll build out character pages, theme pages, plot threads, and keep a running overview of the book as you read.

---

## Query

Ask me anything about your wiki and I'll synthesize an answer from the accumulated pages — with citations. Good answers get filed back into the wiki so your explorations compound over time.

Examples:
- *"What patterns show up in my sleep entries from the last month?"*
- *"How does the theme of identity develop across the chapters I've read?"*
- *"What technologies have I been studying most?"*

---

## Lint

Ask me to health-check the wiki. I'll look for contradictions between pages, orphaned pages, stale claims, missing cross-references, and gaps worth filling with a web search.
